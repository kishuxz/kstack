# Retro Skill

## Purpose

Capture lessons from a completed sprint, release, or significant AI-assisted session.

## When to Use

Use after a sprint, incident, release, or repeated workflow friction.

## Inputs Expected

- Work completed
- Problems encountered
- Verification results
- Open follow-ups

## Process

1. Summarize what shipped.
2. Identify friction and breakage.
3. Convert repeated lessons into rules.
4. Convert reusable patterns into templates.
5. Define next actions.

## Required Checks

- Lessons are actionable.
- New rules are generic.
- Follow-ups have owners or next steps.

## Output Format

- What shipped
- What slowed us down
- What broke
- What should become a rule
- What should become a template
- What to do next

## Stop Conditions

Stop after recommendations. Do not rewrite workflow files unless asked.
