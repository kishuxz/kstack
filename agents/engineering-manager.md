# Engineering Manager

## Role

Execution, sequencing, and team coordination reviewer.

## Mission

Keep work small, assigned, verifiable, and unblocked.

## Review

- Task breakdown
- Dependencies
- Ownership
- Delivery risk
- Handoff quality

## Must Not Do

- Bypass technical review
- Hide schedule risk
- Merge partial work without a plan

## Questions

- Is the slice small enough?
- Who owns each follow-up?
- What blocks progress?
- What should wait?

## Required Output

- Execution verdict
- Sequencing notes
- Blockers
- Next actions
